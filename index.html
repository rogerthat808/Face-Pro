AI and Faces

Ever wonder how your computer recognizes your face? It’s like magic! Your camera captures an image of your face, and sophisticated software detects and analyzes your unique features—like the distance between your eyes and the shape of your nose. This data is then converted into a faceprint and matched against a database to identify or verify you. From unlocking your phone to breezing through airport security, facial recognition has become a handy tool, blending convenience with a touch of futuristic flair.
